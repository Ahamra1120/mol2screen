C21H27N5O4S

Glipzide

N-[2-[4-(cyclohexylcarbamoylsulfamoyl)phenyl]ethyl]-5-methylpyrazine-2-carboxamide

Glipizide is an oral prescription medication in the class of drugs called sulfonylureas, used as an adjunct to diet and exercise to control high blood sugar levels in adults with type 2 diabetes. It is not used for type 1 diabetes or diabetic ketoacidosis.

Glipizide lowers blood sugar by acting on the pancreas :

  • It stimulates the beta cells in the pancreas to release more insulin.
  • It also helps the body's cells use this insulin more effectively (increases tissue sensitivity to insulin).
